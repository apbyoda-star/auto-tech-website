What Is A DVI?

No Surprises. No Pressure.

Gone are the days of a technician trying to describe your vehicle's issues over the phone. During our comprehensive digital inspection, our ASE Certified technicians scrutinize your vehicle bumper-to-bumper and document each finding with real photos.

When the inspection is complete, we send you a detailed report by text or email — so you see exactly what we see, with your own eyes. You can research the findings, ask questions, and decide which repairs to complete now and which to save for later. No pressure, ever.

And the value only grows over time. Each visit builds a documented history of your vehicle's health, so you can track changes, catch small problems before they become expensive ones, and stay ahead of maintenance — not behind it.

  • 25+ point inspection on every single visit — bumper to bumper
  • Real photos document every finding so you see exactly what we see
  • Color-coded results: Green = OK  ·  Yellow = Watch it  ·  Red = Needs attention now
  • Full report delivered by text or email — review it from your phone
  • Know what needs immediate attention and what can wait
  • Includes a courtesy alignment quick-check on our Hunter machine
  • Builds a health history for your vehicle — track changes visit over visit
  • 100% complimentary — no charge, no request needed
  • Your decision on all repairs — we show you, you choose
