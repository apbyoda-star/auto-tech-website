Remanufactured Engines & Transmissions with a Nationwide Warranty

Auto Tech Services is an authorized Jasper installer. When your engine or transmission is beyond repair, a Jasper remanufactured unit is often the best value available — built to original equipment specifications, tested before it ships, and covered by one of the strongest warranties in the industry.

Jasper has been remanufacturing drivetrain components since 1942. Every unit is rebuilt to meet or exceed the original manufacturer's specifications using updated parts where factory designs had known weaknesses. That means you're not just getting the engine your car came with — you're getting a better version of it.

What the Warranty Covers

  • 3 years or 100,000 miles — whichever comes first
  • Parts and labor — not just the part itself
  • Nationwide coverage — honored at thousands of shops across the country
  • Transferable if you sell the vehicle during the warranty period

Why Remanufactured vs. Used

A used engine or transmission comes with unknown history. You don't know how it was maintained, how many miles it actually has, or what's about to fail. A Jasper unit is remanufactured from the core up — machined, inspected, assembled, and tested. Every one leaves the plant meeting a documented standard. That's a fundamentally different product, backed by a warranty that reflects it.
