BG Products Services

15 premium preventive maintenance services — each one backed by the BG Lifetime Protection Plan.

Auto Tech Services uses BG Products — one of the most trusted names in automotive maintenance chemistry. BG's fluid services remove accumulated deposits, restore protection, and extend the life of your vehicle's most critical systems.

Watch the short videos below to see exactly what each service does and why it matters. When you're ready, give us a call.

  • Backed by the BG Lifetime Protection Plan — covers the serviced system for as long as you own the vehicle
  • Services available for engines, transmissions, fuel systems, brakes, cooling, and more
  • Products not available at parts stores — professional-grade chemistry used by shops, not DIY shelves
  • Each service takes less than an hour and can be added to any regular visit
