What We Do

Expert Auto Services

From routine maintenance to major repairs, our certified team handles it all with honesty and precision.

Maintenance & Brakes
Oil changes, fluid checks, and manufacturer-recommended maintenance schedules — plus pad and rotor replacements, brake fluid flushes, caliper service, and full brake system inspections.
Key Cutting & Programming
Licensed locksmith facility offering key cutting and OEM transponder & proximity fob programming for most makes and models. Lost your only key? We've got you covered.
Engine Repair
Diagnostics, tune-ups, timing belt replacements, gasket repairs, and comprehensive engine rebuilds for all makes and models.
Advanced Electrical Diagnosis
In-depth electrical system diagnostics including wiring faults, sensor failures, charging issues, and complex module troubleshooting beyond standard OBD scanning.
Diesel Repair
Full-service diesel engine repair and maintenance for trucks and commercial vehicles, including fuel system service, injector work, and performance diagnostics.
AC & Heating
Refrigerant recharge, compressor repair, heater core service, and full climate control diagnostics for year-round comfort.
Transmission Service
Fluid changes, filter replacements, clutch repairs, and complete transmission rebuilds for automatic and manual vehicles.
Loaner Cars Available
Drop it off and relax in our clean waiting room with complimentary coffee — or step next door to Gathering Grounds. Loaner vehicles also available. Subject to availability — ask when you schedule.
